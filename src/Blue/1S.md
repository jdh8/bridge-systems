# Natural 1♠

- MIN (~11--13): 4+♠ but not 4333 or 44(xx)
- MAX (~14--15): 5+♠ or unbalanced 4=♠ with possibly a longer minor suit

Inspired by [Twisted 2/1][refx], I swap the natural suits for 2♣♥.

[refx]: https://hackmd.io/@TpKotoba/refx
