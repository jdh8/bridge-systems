# Swapped 2/1

## 2♣ for hearts

| 1♠-2♣ | 5+♥ or FG 4+♥ |
|-------|---------------|
| 2♦!   | (R)
| 2♥    | MIN, 4--5♠, 2--3♥
| 2♠    | MIN, 6+♠, 0--2♥
| 2NT!  | MAX, 6+♠, 3=♥
| 3♣♦!  | MIN SPL, 0--1#, 4+♥, 5+♠
| 3♥    | MIN, 4+♥, 5+♠
| 3♠!   | MIN, 5+♥, 5+♠
| 3NT!  | MAX, 4+♥, 5+♠
| 4♣♦!  | MAX SPL, 0--1#, 4+♥, 5+♠
| 4♥    | MAX, 5+♥, 5+♠

## 2♦ for diamonds

Note that 2♦ is also in charge of the constructive raise.

| 1♠-2♦ | 4+♦, not 4=♥ |
|-------|--------------|
| 2♥!   | (R), MIN 5+♠
| 2♠    | MIN, 4=♠

| 1♠-2♦ 2♠- | MIN, 4=♠ |
|-----------|----------|
| P         | CONST, 4--5♠
| 2NT       | FG
| 3♦        | INV, 6+♦
