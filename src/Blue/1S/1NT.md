# Non-forcing 1NT

Opener passes with balanced minimum.

| 1♠-1NT | CONST--INV, 0--3♠ |
|--------|-------------------|
| 2♣     | 5+♠, 4+♣ or BAL 13--15
| 2♦♥    | 5+♠, 4+#
| 2♠     | 11--13, 6+♠.  13--15, 5=♠
| 2NT!   | 14--15, 6=♠, ≤ ♠AJ9
| 3♣     | 14--15, 5+♣, 4+♠
| 3♦♥    | 14--15, 5+#, 5+♠
| 3♠     | 14--15, 6+♠, AJT+ or 7+♠
| 3NT!   | MAX P/C, 6+♦♣, 4=♠
| 4X     | MAX, 5+#, 6+♠

Just like [1♥-1NT](../1H/1NT.md), the threshold of suit quality is based on
[Binky Points][binky].
