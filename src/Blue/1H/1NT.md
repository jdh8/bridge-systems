# Non-forcing 1NT

Opener may rebid a 3-card minor due to protection from the impossible 2♠.

| 1♥-1NT | CONST--INV, 0--3♠, 0--3♥ |
|--------|--------------------------|
| 2♣      | 3+♣, 5+♥
| 2♦      | UNBAL 3+♦, 5+♥
| 2♥      | 11--13, 6+♥.  13--15, 5=♥
| 2♠      | 14--15, 4+♠, 5+♥
| 2NT     | 14--15, 6=♥, ≤ ♥AJ9
| 3♣♦     | 14--15, 5+#, 4+♥
| 3♥      | 14--15, 6+♥, AJT+ or 7+♥
| 3NT!    | MAX P/C, 6+♦♣, 4=♥
| 3♠, 4♣♦ | MAX, 5+#, 6+♥

The threshold of suit quality is based on [Binky Points][binky]:

>  AQTxxx   0.99   0.65
> ```
