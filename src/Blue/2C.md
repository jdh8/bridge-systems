## The 2♦ relay

This relay asks for 4-card majors and strength.

| 2♣-2♦ | INV+ |
|-------|------|
| 2♥♠   | 11--13, 4+#
| 2NT   | 11--13, 6+♣, 0--3♠, 0--3♥
| 3♣    | 14--15, 6+♣, 0--3♠, 0--3♥
| 3♦♥♠  | 14--15, 4+#, 6+♣

To differentiate between 5-card and 6-card majors, responder directly rebids a
6-card major but relays to ask for 3-card support.
