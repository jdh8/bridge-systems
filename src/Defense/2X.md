## (2♣) X

I don't recommend [Rumpelsohl](../Rubinsohl.md#rumpelsohl) for (2♣) X.  It works
better at (2♦♥♠).  I have a better yet simple plan: Rubens advances.

|---------|-----|
| 2X      | NAT NF

| 3♦!     | INV+ TRF, 5+♥
| 3♥!     | INV+ TRF, 5+♠
| 3♠!     | FG, usually 6+♦
