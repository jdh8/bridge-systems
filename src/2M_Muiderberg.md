# Muiderberg 2M

Muiderberg shows exactly 5 cards in the bid major and a side minor suit.
[Multi 2♦](2D_Multi.md) already takes care of 6+ cards.

| 2M    | PRE, 5+M, 4+m |
|-------|---------------|
| 2♠    | NAT NF
| 2NT!  | INV+, ask for the minor suit
| 3♣!   | P/C
| 3♦!   | INV+, 3+M

| 2M-2NT | INV+ (R) |
|--------|----------|
| 3♣     | MIN, 4+♣ |
| 3♦     | MIN, 4+♦ |
| 3♥!    | MAX, 4+♣ |
| 3♠!    | MAX, 4+♦ |
