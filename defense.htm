Introduction

Welcome to Chen-Pang He (jdh8)'s experimental defensive bidding system! We use the distribution syntax and abbreviations provided by WBF.

http://www.worldbridge.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/Guidetocompletion.pdf

This bidding system is based on Good, Better, Best by Jan Eric Larsson and my improvisation.

Over natural (1X)

  • (1Y)
    • Dbl T/O 11+ HCP or any 17+ HCP
    • 1Z 5+#, 10–17 HCP
    • 1NT 15–17 HCP w/ likely stop
    • 2X 5+#, 12–17 HCP
    • 2Z 5+#, 4–10 HCP
    • 2NT !UNT, 55+ in lowest two unbid suits, 10–15 HCP
    • 3X 7+#, 5–9 HCP
    • 3Y !Gambling, SOL 7+m, at most partial stop
    • 3Z 7+#, 5–9 HCP
    • 3NT !Gambling, SOL 7+m w/ likely stop
  • (1 ♣ )
    • 2 ♣ !Landy, 4+ ♠ , 4+ ♥
      • 2 ♦ !(R), equal preference
  • (1 ♦ )
    • 2 ♦ !Michaels, 5+ ♠ , 5+ ♥
  • (1 ♥ )
    • 2 ♥ !Michaels, 5+ ♠ , 5+m, 10+ HCP
  • (1 ♠ )
    • 2 ♠ !Michaels, 5+ ♥ , 5+m, 10+ HCP

Due to insufficient strength, 1NT by passed hand becomes unusual.

  • (1X)
    • 1NT !UNT, 55+ in lowest two unbid suits
